Focus: Breast Cancer

About JayAwesome Foundation

We exist to reduce breast cancer mortality in Nigeria through awareness, early detection, referrals, and practical treatment support — with clear transparency reporting.

Mission

To reduce breast cancer mortality in Nigeria through early detection, awareness, patient support, and transparent reporting.

Vision

A Nigeria where no one dies from breast cancer due to late detection, stigma, or lack of financial support.

How we work

  • Community outreach for screening and education
  • Partnerships with hospitals and professionals
  • Treatment assistance for verified cases
  • Public transparency summaries and impact updates
