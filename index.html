Our 5 Core Programs

Built around awareness, early detection, patient support, and advocacy.

1) Free Screening Outreach

Community screenings across rural and urban areas, with referrals where needed.

  • Mobile outreaches
  • Referral support
  • Community education
2) Awareness & Education

Education in churches, schools, markets, workplaces, and communities.

  • Self-examination education
  • Early signs awareness
  • Myth-busting
3) Treatment Support Fund

Financial assistance for chemotherapy, surgery, medication, and transport.

  • Verified case support
  • Medication assistance
  • Transport/logistics
4) Survivors Support Network

Peer support, counseling, mentorship, and community for patients and survivors.

  • Support groups
  • Counseling & referrals
  • Mentorship
5) Research & Advocacy

Campaigns and advocacy to improve access, awareness, and outcomes.

  • Policy engagement
  • Partnerships
  • Impact reporting
