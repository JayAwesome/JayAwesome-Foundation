5 Core Programs

Programs

Our programs are designed to reduce late presentation and increase access to support across Nigeria.

1) Free Screening Outreach

Community screenings across rural and urban areas with referrals where needed.

  • Mobile outreaches
  • Referral guidance
  • Education at point of care

2) Awareness & Education

Churches, schools, markets, workplaces, and community hubs.

  • Self-exam education
  • Early signs awareness
  • Myth-busting

3) Treatment Support Fund

Help with chemotherapy, surgery, medication, and transport for verified cases.

  • Verified support
  • Medication assistance
  • Transport/logistics

4) Survivors Support Network

Support groups, counseling referrals, and mentorship for survivors and patients.

  • Peer support
  • Counseling
  • Mentorship

5) Research & Advocacy

Advocacy and partnerships to improve access and outcomes.

  • Policy engagement
  • Stakeholder partnerships
  • Impact reporting
